2025

25th-26th October 2025

IEEE Xtreme 19.0

I participated in IEEE Xtreme 19.0 as both a competitor and a Secretary Team Member in the organizing committee. As part of the OC, I created permission letters, event reports, and VC reports to ensure smooth execution. Competitively, I joined the 24-hour coding challenge with my team, Runtime3xception, marking my second consecutive year. The experience taught me invaluable lessons about teamwork, problem-solving under pressure, and understanding people, making it an unforgettable journey of growth and coding endurance.

I contributed as a Finance Team Member, designing partnership proposals, reaching out to sponsors, and successfully securing sponsorship for the event. This experience enriched my skills in professional communication, corporate engagement, and event planning, providing valuable insight into working with companies and managing financial collaborations.

IEEE Student Branch AGM

The Annual General Meeting of the IEEE Student Branch of the University of Vavuniya was held on 8th October 2025. I had the honor of being appointed as the Public Visibility Team Lead, responsible for ensuring the branch's activities and achievements were effectively communicated across platforms. This role allowed me to showcase leadership, coordinate publicity efforts, and contribute to the professional presentation of our branch during this key event.

23rd August 2025

JamborIEEE

At IEEE Andaharaya 2025, I attended as a delegate, joining participants from six universities: UOV, UOJ, UOM, SLIIT, UoVT, and RUSL. The event featured Mr. Manodya Nabadawewa, SAC Chair, as a Power Speaker, delivering an inspiring session filled with energy, insight, and ideas. Despite the focus on technology and innovation, the experience was also fun and memorable, celebrating talent, culture, and the vibrant IEEE spirit at Rajarata University of Sri Lanka on 10th January 2026.

2nd August 2025

Spogomi World Cup 2025 – Qualifier round

I had the honor of serving as the Delegates Coordinator for the University of Vavuniya at the Spogomi World Cup 2025. Being part of the organizing committee offered an incredible opportunity to contribute to an event that celebrates teamwork, sportsmanship, and global collaboration.

26th June 2025

IEEE SLI Career compass

I had the privilege of serving as one of the speakers at IEEE SLI Career Compass 2025, an inspiring educational outreach organized by the IEEE Student Branch of the University of Vavuniya, in collaboration with IEEE SL Inspire. The event connected me with over 150 enthusiastic students and teachers from three schools, where I guided them in navigating their A/L subject selections, university pathways, and government-supported educational opportunities. This experience was deeply rewarding, empowering young minds, fueling ambition, and bridging the gap between dreams and direction.

31st May 2025

HNB Best Speaker Contest (North-East Region)

On 31st May 2025, I had the honor of being on the judging panel for the HNB Best Speaker Contest – North-East Region, organized in collaboration with the Toastmasters Club of Vavuniya. I contributed in multiple capacities, including serving as the Sergeant-at-Arms, ensuring stage readiness, discipline, and smooth execution throughout the event, and representing the Gavel Club of the University of Vavuniya by leading our student judging delegation. This experience provided exposure to elite public speaking talent, hands-on learning in event coordination, role-based responsibility, judging protocol, and professional collaboration. It was both empowering and rewarding to work alongside Toastmasters and Gavel members, fostering growth, shared learning, and a celebration of effective communication. I am grateful to TM Arulneethan Sir for initiating the collaboration, TM M.I. Shamilah Madam for her guidance and support, and TM Edward Sir for mentorship and energetic leadership throughout the program. Events like this reaffirm the power of shared platforms, voices, and growth, leaving a lasting impression and inspiring many more stages, stories, and speaking moments ahead.

24th May 2025

Awurudhu Dance Performance

During my first year at the University of Vavuniya, I had the wonderful opportunity to participate in the Awurudhu dance performance on 24th May 2025. The event celebrated the vibrant traditions and cultural heritage of the New Year festival, bringing together students from various faculties in a lively and colorful performance. Being part of this dance allowed me to connect with my peers, embrace the spirit of teamwork, and contribute to a memorable celebration that highlighted creativity, energy, and cultural pride. It was an exciting experience that balanced performance, coordination, and joyful expression, making my first-year cultural involvement truly special.

26th April 2025

DevSprint 2025

DevSprint 2025 was an exhilarating tech sprint that brought together students, innovators, and experts from across the tech community. I had the privilege of volunteering as the Secretary Team Lead, overseeing documentation, agendas, and overall coordination alongside my amazing team members. The event featured three immersive tracks: Cloud Computing, Mobile App Development, and Artificial Intelligence, with speakers from the GitHub community Sri Lanka sharing valuable insights and practical knowledge. Working closely with Chamodi Indrejith provided guidance that made this journey even more enriching. This experience strengthened my organizational, leadership, and teamwork skills while offering an incredible opportunity to connect with like-minded tech enthusiasts. The energy, passion, and knowledge shared during the sessions made DevSprint an unforgettable milestone in my 2025 journey.

The Inter Faculty Freshers Championship 2025 Karate showcased discipline, strength, and sportsmanship at the University of Vavuniya.
